Serverless Release Orchestration

The Challenge

Manual release processes were causing bottlenecks, with integration testing taking over 4 hours and delaying client deliverables.

Strategic Action

Architected a fully serverless release pipeline using AWS Lambda. I led the digital transformation of the delivery mechanism, automating workflows to ensure scalability and reliability.

Business Impact

Reduced testing time by 95% (to 10 mins) and ensured 98% uptime for critical tools. This revamped the organization's delivery capability, enabling seamless client releases.
