PRISM — Next-Gen Retail Intelligence

The Challenge

Retail enterprises in APAC were being overwhelmed by sell-side AI tools that optimised for the retailer, not the buyer. The market lacked a credible buy-side intelligence product that could shift the power dynamic while remaining commercially viable.

Strategic Action

As Team Lead on a five-person consulting engagement for IBM, defined the core product concept, buy-side versus sell-side positioning, and willingness-to-pay framework. Conducted primary research across 86 respondents and developed an investor-grade business case and agentic AI roadmap.

Business Impact

Delivered a commercially defensible product strategy with validated market sizing, ROI modeling, and a phased go-to-market plan for APAC retail markets. The engagement produced an investor-grade business plan ready for client presentation.

$250M

Kellogg — Mekong Resilience Fund

The Challenge

Climate-driven salinity intrusion in Vietnam's Mekong Delta was threatening 1.1 million farming families and disrupting agricultural supply chains across Southeast Asia. Institutional investors lacked a credible vehicle to fund climate resilience in the region while generating returns.

Strategic Action

Led strategy and investor framing for a four-person team. Developed the core Supply Chain Insetting concept, a novel mechanism where funds build climate resilience within their own supply chain geography through outcomes-based success fees. Structured a $250M blended finance vehicle and presented to senior investment stakeholders at Morgan Stanley's offices in London.

Business Impact

Reached the global finals as one of 12 teams from 186 globally, and the first NBS team to do so. Post-pitch, a judge independently requested the full proposal for further exploration.
