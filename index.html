My Journey

The Spark

My path began with a paper on vehicular-emission monitoring, igniting a passion to use technology for impactful solutions. This led me to Siemens Healthineers, where I dove into the world of medical imaging and its potential to save lives.

AI Awakening

I led the development of a pioneering AI tool for PET-CT imaging, reducing radiologist effort by 40%. A Post Graduate Diploma in ML & AI from IIIT-Bangalore solidified my expertise, providing a robust foundation in deep learning and model deployment.

Cloud & Scale

At IMO, I drove the evolution of critical clinical tools and architected a serverless QA pipeline on AWS Lambda, cutting testing time by 95% and enhancing product quality. My role as Scrum Master further honed my agile leadership skills.

Leadership & Innovation

Today, I blend deep technical knowledge with product thinking and empathetic leadership to mentor teams, drive innovation, and build the future of AI-powered healthcare.
